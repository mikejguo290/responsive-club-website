Accepting. Earnest. Fun.

Curve is an ultimate frisbee club that play in North London every Sunday afternoon.

We welcome players of all abilities, including complete beginners who want to try something new!

We have a thriving social scene and love to party together!

In season, our experienced coaches run dedicated sessions to train players for competitive ultimate.

Come join us!
