Accepting. Earnest. Fun.

Curve is an ultimate frisbee club that plays in North London every Sunday afternoon.

We welcome players of all abilities, including complete beginners who want to try something new!

We have a thriving social scene and love to party together!

In season, our experienced coaches dedicate training sessions to prepare players for competitive ultimate.
